## Full Description

**Razer changes software. Your bindings stay yours.**

SynapseRescue is a Windows app that gives you full control over every Razer Synapse profile on your machine — without needing Razer's account, cloud, or latest software.

### What it does:

**Profile Scanner**
Automatically finds every .synapse3 and .synapse4 file stored on your PC — AppData, Documents, custom locations. No file gets left behind.

**S3 ↔ S4 Converter**
Switching hardware generations? Convert profiles between Synapse 3 and Synapse 4 in one click. Keep your exact bindings across upgrades.

**Export to JSON**
Clean, readable JSON export. Archive your setup, edit it manually, or import it into your own tools.

**Export to AutoHotkey (.ahk)**
Turn Razer macros into standalone scripts. Run your keybindings on any Windows PC with zero Synapse dependency.

**Export to DockStation**
Bridge to DockStation (virtual Stream Deck). Export profile buttons and actions directly into DockStation format.

**Backup All Profiles**
One ZIP. Every profile on your system. Restore any time — reinstalls, driver updates, hardware swaps won't touch your work.
