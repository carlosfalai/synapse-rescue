Features

Your profiles. Your control.

Scan, convert, export, and back up every Razer Synapse profile on your machine. Works offline. No Razer account needed.

Profile Scanner

Automatically finds every Synapse 3 and Synapse 4 profile stored on your PC — in AppData, Documents, custom locations. Nothing gets missed.

S3 ↔ S4 Converter

Convert profiles between Synapse 3 and Synapse 4 formats in one click. Swap hardware generations without rebuilding your bindings from scratch.

Export to JSON

Export any profile as clean JSON — readable, editable, portable. Archive your setup or import it into your own tools and scripts.

Turn your Razer macros into standalone .ahk scripts. Run your keybindings on any Windows PC without Synapse installed at all.

Backup All Profiles

One-click ZIP backup of every profile on your system. Restore anytime. Never lose your setup to a system wipe, reinstall, or driver update again.
